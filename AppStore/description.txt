Deepen your faith with daily Bible lessons, interactive quizzes, and engaging games designed to help you learn and memorize Scripture.

FEATURES:

• 90+ Curated Lessons
Explore essential passages from Genesis to Revelation. Each lesson includes the full passage, a memory verse, and contextual teaching.

• Interactive Quiz Games
Test your Bible knowledge with 200+ thoughtfully crafted questions. Learn about key events, characters, teachings, and verses.

• Memory Verse Flashcards
Master Scripture with our flashcard system. Flip cards, rate your recall, and track your progress toward memorization.

• Fill in the Blank
Complete passages from memory by selecting the right words. A fun way to internalize key verses.

• Match Verse
Connect verse snippets to their references. Strengthen your ability to locate and recall Scripture.

• Daily Reading Plan
Follow a structured 30-day journey through the Bible's most important passages, or explore lessons at your own pace.

• Progress Tracking
See your streak, completed lessons, and XP earned. Stay motivated as you grow in knowledge.

• Beautiful Design
A warm, inviting interface designed for focused reading and distraction-free learning.

Whether you're new to the Bible or deepening an existing faith, Bible Challenge makes Scripture learning engaging, memorable, and rewarding.
