## Recommended Screenshots (in order)

### Screenshot 1: Home Screen
- Show the Home tab with daily reading card
- Highlight: "Learn the Bible Daily"

### Screenshot 2: Lesson View
- Show a lesson being read
- Highlight: "90+ Curated Lessons"

### Screenshot 3: Quiz Game
- Show quiz question with multiple choice
- Highlight: "Test Your Knowledge"

### Screenshot 4: Memory Verses
- Show flashcard being flipped
- Highlight: "Memorize Scripture"

### Screenshot 5: Explore Tab
- Show lesson categories and Today's Pick
- Highlight: "Explore Any Passage"

### Screenshot 6: Progress/Profile
- Show streak, XP, completed lessons
- Highlight: "Track Your Growth"

## How to Capture Screenshots

### Using Simulator:
1. Run app in Xcode with target device
2. Press Cmd + S to save screenshot
3. Screenshots save to Desktop

### Simulators to Use:
- iPhone 15 Pro Max (6.7")
- iPhone 11 Pro Max (6.5")
- iPhone 8 Plus (5.5")

## Tips

1. **Clear the status bar**: Use clean time like 9:41
2. **Show real content**: Use actual lessons, not placeholder
3. **Highlight features**: Add text overlays in Figma/Photoshop
4. **Consistent style**: Use same colors and fonts as app
5. **Portrait orientation**: All screenshots should be portrait

## Adding Text Overlays

Use a tool like Figma, Canva, or Photoshop to add:
- Feature headline at top
- Device frame (optional)
- Consistent background color (#F5F0E6 or #6B4423)

## App Store Preview Video (Optional)

- 15-30 seconds

- No audio required
