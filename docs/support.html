Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my premium purchase?

Go to Profile > Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Is my reading progress saved?

Yes! Your progress is automatically saved on your device. If you sign in with Google or Email, your progress syncs to the cloud so you can access it on multiple devices.

How do I reset my progress?

You can sign out from Profile and create a new account, or contact us to reset your progress.

What Bible translation do you use?

We use the King James Version (KJV), which is in the public domain and widely respected for its beautiful, traditional language.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! All Bible content is stored locally on your device. You only need internet for signing in and syncing progress.
