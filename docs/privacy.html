Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 24, 2026

Welcome to 30 Day Bible Challenge ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our app.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide you with the best experience:

  • App Usage Data: We store your reading progress, completed lessons, streaks, and preferences locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.
  • Purchase Information: If you make in-app purchases, Apple processes the transaction. We only receive confirmation of your purchase status to unlock premium features.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contact lists
  • Photos or media
  • Browsing history
  • Any data for advertising purposes

Data Storage

All your reading progress and app data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload your personal data to any external servers. Your data stays on your device and is protected by your device's security features.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit: For processing in-app purchases. Apple's privacy policy applies to these transactions.
  • Apple WidgetKit: For home screen widgets. Widget data is stored locally using App Groups.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) requirements.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it:

  • Delete the app to remove all data
  • Use your device's settings to manage app permissions
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
